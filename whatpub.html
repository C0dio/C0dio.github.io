WhatPub

As the UK's largest and most influential pub-going consumer organisation, CAMRA has its own online pub guide, with information sourced from real pub-goers.

The guide at WhatPub can be used to search for pubs across the UK and then look up details such as opening times, descriptions, facilities and of course details of the real ale and cider on offer.

WhatPub complements the CAMRA Good Beer Guide: there's no way of searching specifically for Good Beer Guide pubs so you'll still need the book or Mobile App to find the very best real ale pubs.

How you can help!

If you are a CAMRA member, you can access WhatPub now, search for pubs you know and suggest any improvements or corrections by contacting our WhatPub coordinator Neil Warnock.
